| ----------------- | -------------- | ---------------------------- |

| `Alt + grave` | 切換到上一個「活動(Activity)」 | `switch to previous activity=` |
| `Win + Tab` | 切換到下一個「活動(Activity)」 | `switch to next activity=`  |
